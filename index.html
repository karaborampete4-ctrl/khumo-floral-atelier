About Khumo

Founded in the heart of Cape Town's vibrant floral heritage, Khumo Floral Atelier is a sanctuary where South Africa's wild beauty meets unparalleled craftsmanship.

Our atelier draws inspiration from the fynbos kingdoms of the Cape and the majestic proteas of the Drakensberg, transforming indigenous flora into sculptural expressions of luxury and abundance.

With a legacy of serving South Africa's most prestigious estates and events—from the opulent vineyards of Stellenbosch to the skyline galas of Sandton—Khumo crafts bespoke installations that whisper stories of resilience and elegance.

Every creation is a collaboration between nature's artistry and our meticulous vision, ensuring timeless pieces that elevate your most cherished moments.
